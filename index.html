Questions

Not insurance. Something better.

Hannah is a unique, Membership-based veterinary care model built around a long-term care relationship.

Is Hannah Pet insurance?

No. Hannah is not insurance. Hannah Members enroll their Pets in Total Lifetime Care™ plans for covered care through Hannah Pet Hospital.

What does the monthly fee cover?

Total Lifetime Care™ plans include a wide range of covered medical and behavior care through Hannah, including preventive care, illness and injury care, emergency support, dental care, diagnostics, medications, and more.

How do I get started?

Tell us about your Pet, and a Membership Coordinator will help you understand whether Hannah Membership is the right fit.
